INTRODUCTION
Tesla, the electric vehicle company, has captivated global attention over the past decade. Its innovative automotive design, cutting-edge technology, and Elon Musk's leadership have transformed Tesla into more than just a car manufacturer—it is now a symbol of transportation's future and sometimes wealth. This paper will assess whether Tesla truly merits its considerable hype by examining its tech breakthroughs, market success, and long-term prospects in the electric vehicle industry.
